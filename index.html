About

Transforming complexity into
competitive advantage

My work connects strategy, processes, people, and performance to turn complex operational challenges into measurable and sustainable business outcomes.

I am a Chemical Engineer and transformation leader. In less than 10 years, I evolved from trainee to executive manager across four multinationals in the manufacturing industry, leading operations and increasingly complex teams.

Throughout this journey, I have led over 50 transformation initiatives in multinational companies, delivering measurable gains in productivity, quality, safety, availability, operational efficiency, and business value generation.

Key results include reducing operational inefficiency from 12% to 2% at Michelin through process restructuring and performance management; increasing operational capacity by 130% at Moove using VOC and Design Thinking for process redesign; and reducing scrap by 50% at Knauf through BPMN, Lean Manufacturing, and DMAIC.

My approach combines strategic thinking, analytical capability, and a strong execution orientation. I use Lean Manufacturing, Six Sigma Green Belt, BPMN, DMAIC, Design Thinking, and VOC as means to generate impact — never as an end in itself. I believe excellent processes only produce consistent results when supported by a strong culture, prepared leadership, and engaged teams.
